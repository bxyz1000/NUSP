Privacy Policy

Your Data, Clearly Explained.

Effective date: May 23, 2026

What We Collect

SF NUSP may collect the information you provide through the Join Us and Yuvaad registration forms, including your name, age, occupation, institution, city, mobile number, email address, and how you heard about us.

How We Use It

We use submitted information to review interest in SF NUSP, manage Yuvaad registrations, respond to support requests, and share updates related to programs, events, and participation.

  • To contact applicants and participants
  • To organize events and registrations
  • To improve communication and support
  • To maintain internal participation records

Website Tracking

This codebase does not include advertising trackers or analytics cookies by default. Because no non-essential tracking is configured here, a cookie notice is not currently required for this static site implementation.

Retention and Rights

Personal information should be retained only as long as needed for registration, outreach, support, and compliance purposes. If SF NUSP begins serving users in regions where GDPR or similar laws apply, deletion, correction, and access workflows should be honored through the official support channel.

Policy Updates

SF NUSP may update this policy as the website or data workflows evolve. The latest version should always remain published at this URL.
